Slack Clone "Slacky"

TypeScript | Angular | SCSS | Firestore | RxJS

This is a Slack clone. Write text or send images to other people in different channels or direct messages. It is also possible to answer in threads, search posts and edit your profile. Thanks to RxJS Observables and Change Listeners from Firestore, this is a live-chat-application that renders changes immediately in the UI as soon as modifications in channels or direct messages have taken place.
